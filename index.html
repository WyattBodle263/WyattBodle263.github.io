Research Project Sleep App & Sensor - July 2023

I had the privilege of leading the team in the development of a groundbreaking sleep improvement application. Leveraging Flutter, we ensured a cross-platform, aesthetically pleasing, and seamless user experience, accessible to both Android and iPhone users.

Amazon Alexa Controlled Robot

During our EGR101 project, my group successfully built an Alexa-controlled car using an Arduino Uno, an ESP to facilitate the Wi-Fi connection, and a motor controller. The car was able to move forward, backward, left, and right, all controlled by voice commands given through an Alexa device. We encountered several challenges during the project, such as ensuring stable Wi-Fi connectivity and learning the Arduino programming language as well as UI, but ultimately overcame them through teamwork and perseverance.

VEX-U Lancer Robot - September 2022

For the competition, I was on the software development team but also assisted the mechanical team with repairs and implementing new parts to solve problems. With only 30 minutes between each run, it was crucial to work efficiently and effectively to make any necessary adjustments to the robot. Through these experiences, I gained a deeper understanding of the mechanical aspects of robotics and the importance of collaboration between the computer and mechanical teams.

PK Shootout App - January 2019

PK Shootout is an enjoyable app that puts your penalty kick skills to the test against a virtual goalkeeper. Swipe to shoot the ball and see if you can score past the keeper who dives or stays in the middle. Swipe one of three ways to choose your direction and the faster you swipe the harder your kick. The app brings the game to life and creates an immersive soccer atmosphere. Whether you're a die-hard soccer fan or just looking for a fun way to pass the time, PK Shootout is the perfect app for you.

Swish Stats App - May 2019

Swish Stats is the must-have app for basketball players who want to track their performance and compare it with their opponent. Whether you're playing around the world, free throws, or 1v1 basketball competitions, Swish Stats has got you covered. With this app, you can easily track your scores, 3 pointers, free throws, and more, and see how you stack up against your opponent. The app provides an easy way to see who's in the lead and who needs to step up their game. Swish Stats also lets you save your game history, so you can see how you've progressed over time.
