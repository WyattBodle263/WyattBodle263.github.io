Recreation Intern - California Baptist University

August 2021 - April 2024

I played a pivotal role in orchestrating and overseeing the day-to-day operations of a substantial portfolio of more than 35 intramural sports leagues. This responsibility involved meticulous planning, coordination, and execution to ensure the smooth functioning of these leagues.

Additionally, I had the privilege of planning and managing two grand-scale intramural championship events, each drawing an impressive attendance of over 2000 enthusiastic participants and spectators. The successful execution of these events required meticulous preparation, from organizing pre-parties to overseeing the actual games, creating an unforgettable experience for all attendees.

Furthermore, I contributed to the management of three additional championship events, with the most significant of these drawing an astonishing crowd of nearly 5000 individuals. This experience not only tested my organizational and leadership skills but also showcased my ability to handle high-stakes and high-attendance affairs with efficiency and professionalism.

In addition to league and championship management, I co-managed 15 one-day tournaments and events, each hosting up to 150 participants. These events required precise planning, logistics coordination, and on-site management to ensure the satisfaction of all attendees.

My role also extended to the administrative aspects of event management. I operated in an office setting, which involved liaising with and securing commitments from more than 20 vendors. This encompassed handling the necessary paperwork and documentation to ensure the seamless execution of events.

One of my key responsibilities was the compilation of game summaries, which entailed reporting scores, updating team schedules, and maintaining individual and team statistics for an impressive total of over 240 games. This meticulous record-keeping was crucial for tracking the progress and achievements of participating teams and players.

In summary, my tenure in event and sports management has been characterized by hands-on experience, meticulous planning, and a knack for handling both the operational and administrative aspects of large-scale sporting events. I take immense pride in my ability to orchestrate successful events, manage diverse logistics, and maintain comprehensive records, all while ensuring a memorable experience for participants and spectators alike.
