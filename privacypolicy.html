Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the iOS application DrinkShelf, developed by Wyatt Bodle and available via the App Store.

Information Collection

DrinkShelf does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app is designed to be used without requiring any account creation or personal data entry.

Local Storage Only

All bar ingredient data and customizations are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone and is not transmitted to any servers.

No Third-Party Services

DrinkShelf does not use third-party analytics tools, advertising networks, or external SDKs that collect data.

Security

Since DrinkShelf does not collect or transmit user data, there are no privacy risks associated with data breaches or external access.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes in the future, it will be posted at https://wyattbodle.com/privacy.html.

Contact

For questions or concerns, please contact Wyatt Bodle at wyatt@wyattbodle.com.
